# Linear Regression from Scratch

## Why I built it from scratch
I knew the math or thought I did. I could write the formulas, follow the derivations, tick the boxes. But I couldn't *see* it. Writing the logic myself, from the gradient down to the weight update, was the first time the math stopped being symbolic and started meaning something.

## What surprised me
That it actually works. Not in a "the test passed" way: in a deeper way. The same equation I derived on paper is quietly running inside systems that predict what you buy, what you watch, what your body might do next. It's not bookish at all. It's everywhere.

## Who this is for
If you're early in your ML journey and you've done the courses, you understand the theory on paper, but something still feels hollow, this is for you. Not a tutorial. Just one person showing their work.
